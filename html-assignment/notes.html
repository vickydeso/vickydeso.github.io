Paragraph Tag
The most commonly used tag; creates a paragraph of text with spaces before and after it
Highlighting Tag
Highlights a string of text
Emphasis Tag
Italizes a string of text for emphasis
Important Tag
Bolds a string of text for more emphasized information
Deleted Content Tag
