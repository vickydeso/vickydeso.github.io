HTML Tags

<h1></h1>
Creates a section heading with h1 being the most important and h6 being the least important hierarchically
<p></p>
The most commonly used tag; creates a paragraph of text with spaces before and after it
<mark></mark>
Highlights a string of text
<em></em>
Italicizes a string of text to indicate emphasis and importance
<strong></strong>
Bolds a string of text for more emphasized, important information
<hr>
Creates a horizontal line to separate content; not to be confused with a decorative line
