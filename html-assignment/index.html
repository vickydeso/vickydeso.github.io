About Me

Hi! My name is Vicky. I'm a student currently in my 1st year of the IMD program at Algonquin College. I'm very passionate about design and illustration; my dream is to work professionally designing brands, merchandise, and advertisements as a graphic designer. In my free time, I love:

  • drawing
  • listening to music
  • hanging out with my cat

I've been the creative type since childhood, but started to consider a career in the field of design during high school when I took my first graphic design class. I quickly became infatuated with designing layouts, using different typefaces, and creating art that was practical and had purpose; it felt so rewarding to use my creativity to solve design problems and fulfill the needs of people. I'm super excited to keep learning and improve my abilities to bring me closer to my dream of becoming a graphic designer!
